How it works

Three steps, zero friction.

Pick your model

Choose Small for speed or Large for maximum accuracy. Praat downloads Whisper once and runs it entirely on your hardware — Metal on Mac, Vulkan on Windows.

Press & talk

Hold your global shortcut and speak naturally. Praat listens with Voice Activity Detection — it only captures when you're actually talking, not silences.

Text appears instantly

Release the shortcut and your transcription is pasted exactly where your cursor sits — in any app, any text field, any editor. Zero copy-paste needed.

Questions

Everything you might wonder about.

Is it really free? +

Yes. Praat is MIT-licensed open source. Download it, use it, share it with anyone. No subscription, no account, no paywall — ever.

Does my voice get sent anywhere? +

No. Transcription happens locally on your device using Whisper models. Praat makes exactly one network call — to download the model file once on first launch. After that, it works completely offline.

macOS says the app is damaged — what do I do? +

macOS Gatekeeper blocks apps that aren't code-signed with a paid Apple Developer certificate. Right-click the app → Open → Open Anyway. You only need to do this once. Or run xattr -cr /Applications/Praat.app in Terminal.

Windows SmartScreen is blocking the installer. +

Click "More info" → "Run anyway." This happens because Praat doesn't have a paid code-signing certificate. The app is open source — you can review every line at the GitHub link above.

Which model should I pick? +

Turbo is the best default for most people — fast and highly accurate. Use Small on older machines. Use Large if you want the absolute best quality and have a capable GPU.

What permissions does Praat need? +

Microphone access (to hear you) and, on macOS, Accessibility permission (to paste text into other apps). That's it — nothing else is requested.
